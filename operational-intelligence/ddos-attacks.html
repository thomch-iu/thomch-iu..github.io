DDoS Attacks


Discovered Intelligence - Dissemination

  • Tactics used to lower the probability of DDoS attacks should be shared amongst all staff because it is a group effort from all employees.
  • Group meetings (virtual or in person), emails, etc.
  • As the cybersecurity unit you should monitor passwords, firewall, and ports because these are the typical channels that DDoS attacks are executed

Discovered Intelligence - Course of Action

  • Mandatory company wide standards should be created amongst all employees to:
    • Create stronger passwords and enforce MFA for access to sensitive data, valuable assets, etc.
    • Frequent port and firewall monitoring

Discovered Intelligence - Iterative Challenges

  • To prevent future DDoS attacks investments in strong network security
    • Anti Virus/Anti Malware software - helps detect and remove malware and viruses
    • Firewall and intruder detection systems - would scan the traffic at each firewall barrier
